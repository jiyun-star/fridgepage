Academic Title: Ingredient Recognition Recipe Recommendation App

Commercial Title: Fridgemate

Project Description:
This project is a mobile-friendly web application that allows users to take and upload photos of ingredients in their refrigerator or on their table. The AI system automatically recognizes the ingredients in the photo and displays them to the user. Based on the identified ingredients, the app recommends various recipes that can be made using those items. This helps users cook easily with what they have at home and reduces food waste.
